What is Coolmov ?

Coolmov interface showing Stranger Things and more shows in a mosaic display. Coolmov is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device.

Depending on your plan, you can also download TV shows and movies to your Android phone or tablet, iPhone, iPad, or Google Chromebook device and watch without an internet connection.

If you're already a member and would like to learn more about using Coolmov, visit Getting started with Netflix.
